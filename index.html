Biography

Temetrius Jamel "Ja" Morant, born August 10, 1999, in Dalzell, South Carolina, is one of the most electrifying young players in the NBA. Known for his explosive athleticism and creative playmaking, he was drafted 2nd overall by the Memphis Grizzlies in 2019 and quickly became the face of the franchise.
